Rubik's Cube




Information:

Use mouse to orbit around the cube.
Cannot start another transformation while one is already in progress.
All cube transformations are performed with the visible face as reference.

[Arrows] Rotate up/down/left/right
([,]) and [.] Rotate (anti)clockwise
[f r u l b d] Corresponding face rotation
[Shift] + [f r u l b d] Inverse face rotation
[Space] Shuffle cube
